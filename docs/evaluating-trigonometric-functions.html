    Definition 2.4.3.

    On the unit circle, positive angles are measured counterclockwise from the x-axis. Angles greater than \(2 \pi\) are measured by completing one complete rotation, and continuing past the x-axis. Negative angles are measured clockwise from the x-axis. Angles less than \(-2 \pi\) are measured by completing one complete rotation in the counterclockwise direction and continuing past the x-axis.
